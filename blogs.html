SMALL HUMAN SOLUTIONS TO BIG HUMAN PROBLEMS

January 2k21

When was the last time you felt alone? Or more than alone - when was the last time you felt lonely? Loneliness can creep up in small ways: when you work alone and realise you haven’t looked another human being in the eye for four days straight; when you look online and see pictures of your friends hanging out without you; when you text with friends every day but can never find the time to meet up and have a proper heart to heart. It’s the small things that can make us feel lonely but the effect can be profound.
